Crit It

Built in 36 hours from scratch as a personal project, Crit It is an easy critiquing platform for art students! Professors can set up a course page and add their students to the course. Once added, students can post their art assignments to the course page. Students in the class can like and comment, or "critique," their peers' artwork. Professors and teaching assistants can also comment on the various posts within the class, and their comment will be highlighted to show their ranking as either a "Professor" or "TA." Each student has their own profile with their email address, and their history of posts.

Here are quick screen recordings of the functionality of Crit It from a student's perspective and a professor's perspective. If you'd like to see the source code and wireframes, just ask! Built using PHP, HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, Jquery, and MySQL.
